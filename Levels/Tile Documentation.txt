The rightmost four columns in an image determine which tiles are available in that level.

If column 1 is white for any given row, the rest of the information will not be read on that row.

Left to right:

Column 1: tile type
	White none		"ERROR"
	Black turn		"turn"
	Red push		"push"
Column 2: tile color
	Red			"red"
	Green			"green"
	White general	"gen"
Column 3: direction
	White up		"up"
	Black right		"right"
	Red down		"down"
	Green left		"left"
Column 4: durability
	White one		"1"
	Black two		"2"
	Red three		"3"
	Green four		"4"
	Blue infinite	"-1"
